DOC Control Flutter

The app developed in Flutter is a smart home automation app based on IoT technology. The app is designed to communicate with a backend service and access various cameras, doors, and other smart devices within a user's home. The app utilizes the GET-X dependency injection library to manage and inject dependencies throughout the application.

One of the main features of the app is its ability to control and monitor multiple smart devices from a single interface. The app supports cameras, smart door locks, and other IoT devices. Users can view live video feeds from their cameras, lock or unlock their doors, and control various other smart devices from within the app.

The app is designed to be user-friendly and intuitive, with a clean and simple interface. Users can easily navigate through the app and access the various features and functions. The app is also highly customizable, with the ability to create custom profiles and settings for individual devices and rooms.

One unique aspect of the app is its integration with a Kotlin/Android home-screen widget. This widget provides users with quick and easy access to the most commonly used features and functions of the app, such as camera feeds and door locks. The widget is designed to be lightweight and responsive, with minimal impact on system resources.

Overall, the app is designed to provide users with a seamless and convenient smart home automation experience. By leveraging the power of IoT technology and a user-friendly interface, the app aims to simplify the process of managing and controlling multiple smart devices within a user's home.
